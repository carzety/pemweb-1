Gili Trawangan, Lombok

Gili Trawangan is the largest of the three small islands or gilis located northwest of Lombok. Trawangan is also the only dyke whose height above sea level is quite significant. With a length of 3 km and a width of 2 km, Trawangan has a population of about 800 people.

Hutan Kota, Jakarta

The capital city of Jakarta is indeed famous for its high level of community mobility. Millions of vehicles rush back and forth every day and cause high pollution in this city. It can be said that it is very difficult to get clean and fresh air here.

Curug Cikuwulung, Bogor

Unlike other waterfalls, Curug Cikuluwung has beautiful blue water.In fact, some say that this place is similar to the Indonesian version of the Grand Canyon with flowing water. This is due to the beautiful rock cliffs on either side of the river.
